AutoDescribe

RAG + Human-in-the-Loop Product Description Generator

Live Demo View PRD

Problem Statement

E-commerce platforms struggle with creating consistent, high-quality product descriptions at scale, leading to poor user experience and reduced conversion rates. Manual content creation is time-intensive, inconsistent, and doesn't scale with growing product catalogs.

Solution Approach

Developed an AI-powered system combining Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with human-in-the-loop validation to generate contextually relevant product descriptions while ensuring quality and brand consistency.

Key Innovation:

The hybrid approach leverages AI for speed and scale while maintaining human oversight for quality control, ensuring each description meets brand standards and customer expectations.

Use Case

E-commerce platforms with large product catalogs need to generate consistent, high-quality product descriptions at scale. AutoDescribe enables content teams to maintain brand voice while dramatically reducing manual effort through AI-powered generation with human oversight.
